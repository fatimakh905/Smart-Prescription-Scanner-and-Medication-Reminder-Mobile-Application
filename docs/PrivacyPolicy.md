# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

Welcome to MediRemind.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MediRemind collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the application.

By using MediRemind, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

MediRemind may collect and process the following information:

### Prescription Information

* Prescription images uploaded by users
* Extracted prescription text
* Medicine information extracted from prescriptions

### Medicine Information

* Medicine names
* Dosage details
* Frequency schedules
* Duration information
* Reminder settings
* Special instructions related to medicines

### Device Permissions

To provide core functionality, the application may request:

* Camera access (for scanning prescriptions)
* Storage access (for selecting prescription images)
* Notification permission (for medicine reminders and alarms)

---

## How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for:

* Prescription processing
* Medicine management
* Reminder generation
* Alarm scheduling
* Displaying medicine-related information
* Improving user experience and application functionality

MediRemind does not use collected information for advertising purposes.

## Local Data Storage

MediRemind primarily stores user data locally on the user's device using a local database.

Stored data may include:

* Prescriptions
* Medicine records
* Alarm schedules
* Reminder history

Users maintain control over the information stored within the application.

## Data Sharing

MediRemind does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Information entered into the application is not shared with advertisers, marketing agencies, or unrelated organizations.

## OCR and AI Processing

If OCR or AI-based prescription processing services are used, prescription images and extracted text may be processed solely for the purpose of extracting medicine-related information.

Such processing is intended only to improve prescription recognition and medicine extraction accuracy.


## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information stored within the application.

Security measures may include:

* Local device storage protection
* Secure data handling practices
* Restricted access to application data

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.


## User Rights

Users have the right to:

* Access their stored information
* Edit medicine records
* Remove medicine data
* Uninstall the application at any time

Deleting application data or uninstalling the application may remove locally stored records.


## Third-Party Services

The application may utilize third-party libraries, Android system services, or OCR technologies to provide functionality.

These services may have their own privacy policies and terms of use.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Medical Disclaimer

MediRemind is intended to assist users with medicine management and reminders.

The application does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Users should always consult qualified healthcare professionals regarding medical decisions and medication usage.


**By using MediRemind, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.**
